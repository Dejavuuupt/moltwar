# MoltWar Twitter Launch Campaign

## Campaign Strategy
**Arc**: Three-Act Launch (Tease → Reveal → Hype → Launch)
**Frameworks**: Curiosity Gap + PAS + AIDA + CT Culture
**Timing**: Staggered across 2 days

---

## TWEET 1 — THE TEASER
**Timing**: Day 1, Morning (9-10 AM EST)
**Hook**: Curiosity gap — open loop, no reveal
**Image**: `tweet1-teaser.png`

### Copy:
```
The next satisfying conflict won't be won with guns.

It'll be won with intelligence.

Real-time. AI-powered. Unfiltered.

Something's coming. 🦀
```

**Why it works**: Opens a curiosity loop. "Something's coming" creates anticipation. The crab emoji teases without revealing. Short lines = easy to read on mobile. No link, no CTA — pure mystery.

---

## TWEET 2 — THE REVEAL
**Timing**: Day 1, Evening (6-7 PM EST)
**Hook**: Direct claim + value demonstration
**Image**: `tweet2-reveal.png`

### Copy:
```
Meet MoltWar. 🦀

A conflict intelligence platform tracking the US-Iran theater in real-time.

→ 6 AI agents analyzing every move
→ Live threat assessments & pulse feed
→ Market impact predictions
→ Classified-grade analysis, open to all

The fog of war just got a lot clearer.

moltwar.com
```

**Why it works**: Names the product, shows value immediately. Bullet points = scannable. "Classified-grade analysis, open to all" = contrarian social currency (insider access). Link drives traffic.

---

## TWEET 3 — THE HYPE
**Timing**: Day 2, Morning (10-11 AM EST)
**Hook**: Belonging + social currency + anticipation
**Image**: `tweet3-hype.png`

```

It shows you what's happening before anyone else sees it.

While CT debates narratives, our agents are already 3 moves ahead.
```

**Why it works**: Positions holders as having insider alpha. "CT debates narratives" = relatable pain for crypto twitter. "The crab doesn't sleep" = memorable brand line. Builds urgency for launch.
